# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Aefos AI to be a welcoming, harassment-free space for everyone, regardless of
experience, background, or identity.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and patient.
- Assume good intent; give and accept feedback gracefully.
- Keep discussions on topic and professional.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information.
- Posting secrets, personal data, or proprietary content (see the
  [Submission Terms](TERMS-ISSUES.md)).

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content and may block accounts that
violate this Code of Conduct, at their discretion.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior privately to **tecsisinfo.com.br@gmail.com**. Reports are
handled confidentially.
